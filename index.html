About Badillo

Badillo is a Mexican-American hip hop artist from Oceanside, California. Growing up, he was inspired by artists such as Eminem, 50 Cent, 2pac, The Weeknd and Drake. He started out writing poems and lyrics to songs that eventually pushed him to produce his first song in early 2018.

Expect to see an album dropping in February 2020, meanwhile enjoy some more singles coming soon.
